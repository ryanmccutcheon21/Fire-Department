    SUPPRESSION

    The Fort Smith Fire Department is responsible to prepare for, respond to, and control all types of fires within the City of Fort Smith. The department coordinates firefighting activities, including suppression in rural and urban firefighting. Firefighters are given progressive training in the techniques and science of fire control as they advance through their careers.

    BOMB SQUAD

    The Fort Smith Fire Department Bomb Squad is an accredited Explosive Ordinance Disposal (EOD) Team as defined by the FBI Hazardous Devices School and the National Bomb Squad Commanders Advisory Board. The Bomb Squad was formed in 2004, and currently has six certified FBI Bomb Technicians, who are also part of the HAZMAT Team.

    DIVE TEAM

    Our divers are certified Public Safety Divers. Each diver is trained to descend to the bottom of a body of water to search in low to zero visibility to recover bodies and evidence. Public Safety Divers work alone underwater following overlapping search patterns as directed by their tender. The tenders are the "brains" of the operation. They are on shore on the other end of the diver's rope. The diver and tender communicate with each other using predetermined rope tug signals; the tender is the one who directs the search and keeps track of the diver's air supply. Searches can be time consuming, depending on the size of the search object, but at the end of the search there should be nothing in the search area that has not been touched by the diver.
